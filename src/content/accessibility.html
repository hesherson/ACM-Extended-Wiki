02

Colourblind Correction: Protanopia, Deuteranopia & Tritanopia

Applies to everything the addon draws, not just the menu: ventilator panel and its alarm tiers, EMMA capnograph, blood cooler states, chest seal states.

Why a transform, not a second paletteDesign

Hand-authoring an alternate palette per mode would mean four versions of every colour in the addon, and every colour added later would have to be authored four times or silently fall back to the wrong one.

A transform applied at the point of use is much harder to forget, and anything added later inherits it for free.

These are corrections, not simulationsImportant

Each mode takes the difference a given deficiency cannot resolve and pushes it onto an axis that deficiency still has. They are corrections applied for the viewer, rather than simulations of what a colourblind person sees.

For protanopia and deuteranopia that means carrying the red-green difference on blue. For tritanopia it means carrying the blue-yellow difference on red-green.

Measured against the actual palette, by simulating what each deficiency perceives and checking the closest pair of colours: protanopia improves from 0.063 to 0.185, deuteranopia from 0.109 to 0.162, tritanopia from 0.087 to 0.164.

Greys, whites and blacks pass through untouched, because an achromatic colour has no difference to redistribute.

The alarm tiers get particular attention, because red HIGH against yellow MEDIUM is the pair red-green deficiency collapses and it is the most safety critical distinction in the addon. It widens in all three modes and is protected against being dimmed toward the panel background.
